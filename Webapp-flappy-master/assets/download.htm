Shanghai. Bund at Sunset

Shanghai. Bund at Sunset

Share this post:

FacebookTwitterRedditGoogle+TumblrPinterestEmail

Probably it’s one of the most well-known skylines in the world – Pudong district in Shanghai seen from the Bund promenade, waterfront area which runs along the western bank of the Huangpu River.

This is one of the most crowded places I’ve ever seen – there were literally thousands of people along the river waiting for iconic Oriental Pearl Tower (tall, pink one in the left of the image) to be lit up in the early evening. This made taking the photos, especially long exposure ones, very challenging as every few seconds someone kicked or pushed my tripod despite the fact I put it in such place that it wasn’t a problem for anyone.

Anyway, I managed to capture some nice images like the one in this post. The scene looks best in long exposure in my opinion as otherwise, water is very distracting.
